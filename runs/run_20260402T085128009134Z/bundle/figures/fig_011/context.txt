To solve this, sectorization can be performed. In the case of a sectorized cell, each cell is divided into either 3 or 6 equal parts or sectors. It means using 3 or 6 directional antennas at the base station instead of a single omnidirectional antenna to reduce the number of co-channel interferences. In the proposed antenna, we sectorize the cell into 3 parts, which means the antenna’s radiation angle should be 120°.
